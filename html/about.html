About us

Welcome to Kovels, your number one source for electric skateboards. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of electric skateboards, with a focus on speed, range and comfort.

Founded in 2018 by Vairis Kovels, Kovels has come a long way from its beginnings in Riga. When Vairis first started out, his passion for eco-friendly electric skateboards drove him to quit day job so that Kovels can offer you the world's best electric skateboard. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into my own website.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
